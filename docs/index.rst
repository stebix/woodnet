Who
----

This code infrastructure arose as part of a research project between the
`Fraunhofer EZRT <https://www.iis.fraunhofer.de/de/ff/zfp.html>`_
`Experimental Physics V of the University of Würzburg <https://www.physik.uni-wuerzburg.de/ep5/>`_,
and
`Thünen Institute of Wood Research <https://www.thuenen.de/de/fachinstitute/holzforschung>`_.
